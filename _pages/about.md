Hello! I am **Yunseo Lee**, an undergraduate student at **Seoul National University (SNU)**, double majoring in **Chemical & Biological Engineering** and **Computer Science & Engineering**.

My research focuses on the intersection of **materials science, chemistry, and machine learning**, with an emphasis on developing data-driven approaches to understand and design complex systems.

---

## 🔬 Research Interests

- AI-driven materials discovery  
- 4D TEM-based structural analysis  
- Molecular machine learning  
- Data-driven scientific modeling and automation  

---

## 🧪 Current Work

I am currently involved in research projects including:

- **4D TEM analysis of platinum nanoparticles**  
  → reconstructing dynamic 3D atomic structures and analyzing structural evolution  

- **Machine learning for molecular property prediction**  
  → developing models for drug-related tasks using graph-based and representation learning approaches  

- **Computational workflow design**  
  → building scalable pipelines for processing large-scale experimental data  

---

## 🎯 Research Vision

I am interested in bridging **experimental observations and computational intelligence**, aiming to build integrated frameworks that combine:

- Experimental data (e.g., microscopy, spectroscopy)  
- Machine learning models  
- Physical understanding  

to enable **next-generation materials and molecular discovery**.
